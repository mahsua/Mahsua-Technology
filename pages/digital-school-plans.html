Frequently Asked Questions

How does the per-student pricing work?

We calculate pricing based on your total student enrollment. You'll be billed annually for the number of students enrolled at the beginning of the academic year.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. We'll prorate the cost difference for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Is training included with these plans?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training for your staff with all our plans. The Complete Digital School and Enterprise plans include priority training sessions.
